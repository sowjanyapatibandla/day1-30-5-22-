    • In ReactJS, every component craetion process involves various lifecycle methods. These lifecycle methods are not very complicated and called at various points during a component's life. the lifecycle of the component is divided into four phases . they are: 1.Initial phase 2.Mounting phase 3.Updating phase 4.Unmounting phase Each phase contain some lifecycle methods that are specific to the particular phase.
